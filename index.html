Week 9

Election Edition: Senpais Scoop!
Left wingers, right wingers, and everyone in between….
Welcome to the third edition of Senpais Scoop! Week Nine has officially ended just like the hopes of several of our league mates. The race is officially on for the dog cage! With a three headed race between The Promised Land, Let’s Go Golfing, and Ruggs driving class. Each team finds themselves fighting week in and week out to avoid the “doghouse.” Driving Case, with 2 owners, Halsey and EBABY could potentially force the leagues hands to purchase a substantially bigger dog cage and/or another separate cage. Needless to say, each one of these respected teams is “campaigning” for any hope. Last week's game to watch, featuring Enthusiast (Commish) vs. Show (Shieldys) was once again, a not so close matchup. Show, taking his second loss of the season, is looking to bounce back against another struggling team in Snead. For the commish, this was his quest for a third straight win! After another massive trade for star running back Bijan Robinson, the commissioner is continuing his turnaround season!

This week features two games to look out for! Patty Cake, led by owners Zion and Shadique travel east to take on Under Rayted, led by owner Ray. For cake, currently leading the NFC and dominating the league in points scored, is looking to widen the gap and grab the one seed! For Under Rayted, coming off two straight losses and facing some tough bye week decisions, he looks to “flip the house” on his season. The final matchup to watch this week features two 5-4 teams! Peener and Collins (led by owners JJ and Jesse respectively) face off this week in an ever important matchup to potentially determine the fate of their seasons! For both owners, they have struggled all season with injuries and up and down starts. However, both at 5-4 and still find themselves entrenched in playoff contention!

As always, our league is built on the 4 F’s. Family, friends, football and fear! Throughout major life changes, elections, historical events, or anomalies, this league survives. Shoutout to all of you for your commitment and love for not just fantasy but for the friend group!

Much love - Senpai.
